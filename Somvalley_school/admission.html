Admission Process

The school accepts applications forms for admissions up to the end of February each year. Admissions are granted subject to the availability of seats. Applicants will be put on a waiting list if there are limited seats.

You will need :

  • As per CBSE rule, new academic year starts from the month of April of each year.
  • Admissions are depends upon the availability of seats.
  • Candidates must complete 6 years of his/her age till 30th July for primary section.
  • Parents can pay the fees in three installments (quarterly)

Documents needed

  • Original birth certificate (Applicable to only 1st std.)
  • Leaving Certificate (applicable to 2nd to 10th std.)
  • Report card of last attended school
  • Caste certificate if needed
  • Passport size photos of Parents(one each), Students(3 each)
