Pre School

The curriculum is designed to facilitate overall development through development of five main domains

  • Cognitive Development
  • Speech and Language Development
  • Social & Emotional Development
  • Gross Motor Skills Development
  • Fine Motor Skills Development

The subjects are integrated with the latest technology to offer the child a diverse and stimulating fun filled learning aligned ambience.
