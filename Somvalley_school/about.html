Welcome to Some valley international school

Som Valley International School is an English medium school, based on CBSE curriculum. It is situated in Bagaha, West Champaran Bihar.

Som Valley International School focuses on conceptual learning through the best tools available globally for the holistic development of the students.

Som Valley Internatinal School provide a setting where children are seen as individuals, where their needs are identified and responded to and where they are given the best possible chance to fulfil their talents. We encourage children to grow in confidence and self-esteem, to become more independent and learn to take responsibility.

We have designed our curriculum such that it gives your child every opportunity to explore and discover his / her own innate abilities.
